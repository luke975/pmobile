Pumpkin Mobile Messaging Service

PMMS is our new message system for Pumpkin Mobile Paper THiN and newer devices. It utilizes the accounts in our database so if you type in someones L Account email you can message/email them. OS 2 introduced PMMS 2- which uses random encryption each time you send a message that only the recipient has the decryption key to display the message. It does not utilize cellular network if not needed unlike before.

Pumpkin Insta-Charge

Included in our devices since the first phone, you can use a pumpkin to charge your phone! 1 pumpkin will last for 30 years straight of charging, and remember that it only takes 30 seconds to charge!

Pumpkin Charge Card

Charge Card Original

We took a lot of juice from pumpkins, enhanced it, packed it into a card, and got this! Now if you tap for 5 seconds, your device is at 100%! The card also lasts forever because we added some secret ingredients to make it so the Pumpkin Juice reproduces itself.

AUAAS

AUAAS (Advanced and Ultra Advanced Audio System) is the successor to UEQAS, so its basically UEQAS version 2. But instead of using enhanced stereo speakers, we have added surround sound speakers into the laptop hinge / back of tablet. Also surround sound is only in Pumpkin FOLD devices.

UEQAS

UEQAS (Ultra Enhanced Quality Audio System), is our top of the line audio system present in our phones and tablets. We did this by taking our best speakers, making the components smaller, and putting it in our phones!

PUCF

Previously C+ZIP, PUCF (Pumpkin Ultra Compressed File) has a really high compression rate, the same rate as if you compressed a .ZIP (Explaining the previous name, Compression + ZIP). So its really good for making things into small file sizes. This technology is open source, so you can put this technology to say, make a new music file that is super small in file size!
